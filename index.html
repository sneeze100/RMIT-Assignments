    Project Idea

    Overview

    The Project idea that I have decided to work on is a phone-based application that can scan groceries as you add them to your shopping trolley or shopping basket, this application would be designed for all mainstream supermarkets and would be in the same vain as self-service checkouts, imagine the ease of putting your bags into the shopping trolley and the ability to play shopping trolley Tetris without having to unpack it for the cashier or the self-service check out and the only time you need to unpack is when you get home.

    Motivation

    The motivation behind this application is due to the way supermarkets are starting to reduce their pay roles and making you use self-checkout more often than not due to them not opening cashier lanes for their customers, also the benefit of being able to handle the products once during your shopping experience allows you to pack your bags the way you want to only once. Also, the ability to reduce your shopping bill by using available coupons found on the internet, and noting that prices at the moment seem to keep going up the benefit of reducing family's shopping costs are a driving factor.

    Description

    This new shopping experience application will be a one-stop shopping application and will be modern in appearance and also free from advertisements, the application will ask for permission to use your camera function, which will allow for the scanning of barcodes and QR codes for each item. The application will draw on the payment option like apple pay and google pay that you have your phone already set to, with a small price tag of zero dollars you will be able to purchase a shopping trolley scale that will connect to your phone via Bluetooth, this will allow the purchaser to scan fresh food that is normally weighed and allow the weighing scale in the bottom of your trolley to work out the weight of the product as you add it to your trolley.

    Another feature of the application will be the function to use the required supermarket rewards card account and also the ability to search the internet for additional coupons. This function will allow shoppers to reduce their overall shopping bills. Another application is the ability to take a stocktake of your current pantry goods and chilled goods, with the integration of a menu function it will automatically tell you what products you need to purchase to make each meal on your menu. The shopping application will have the ability to remember your last shopping haul and will be able to make suggestions to add to your shopping trolley whilst you are shopping, this function will allow you to broaden your horizons on items that you are purchasing. The application will allow you to conduct your shopping from the comfort of your own home by having a clean and user-friendly user interface, this will move away from the clunky displays that are currently on offer.

    So the supermarket does not lose out to shoplifting the weighing mat will keep track of all items to measure the known weights of standard products that are purchased every day also taking into account the weight of fresh produce, the application will have two-factor authentication for the purchase of fresh produce, the scanning of the fresh produce code and recognition software that will require a photograph of the item to confirm it is what you say it is before self-deleting off your phone to save memory space on your device.

    the application will have additional security by utilizing biometric data and a PIN this will protect your account and stop any malicious purchasing from people that steal your phone or try and hack your account, every endeavor will be taken to protect your personal information with strict adherence to privacy laws.

    Tools and Technologies

    The tools I will use to develop this application will be Xcode 14 and Eclipse for Android Developers these programs provide everything you need to create an application for IOS and Android. And because of my limited knowledge, I will also use a how-to guide on how to program a barcode scan application. The shopping application will be made using a shopping application guide.

    Outcome

    If this project is successful it will allow you to skip the dreaded self-service check out, where you technically do the check out operator job yourself. The application will allow you to conduct the self-serve function but it will become seamless as you will be scanning as you are packing your bags in the shopping trolley. I am sure if this application works as detailed supermarkets will look favorable at this technology as it will reduce the financial cost on the payroll.
